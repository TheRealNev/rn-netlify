When I receive the designs as Sketch Measure files I take a look at the positioning of, and spacing between elements before I start building out a skeleton of the page. Once the initial structure is built, I then add more detail with coloring, images, and links. As for the Locator page a lot of the results rendering was dealt with by JavaScript so the results styling didn't take place until the programming was taken care of. When the pages are looking finished I reopen the Sketch Measure files and make sure that the measurements and sizes match the design for desktop, tablet, and mobile breakpoints.
